Tribute to A.P.J.Abdul Kalam

About A.P.J.Abdul kalam

A.P.J. Abdul Kalam the "Missile Man of India"was born October 15,1931,in Rameswaram,Tamil Nadu.He served as the 11th President of India and was a renowned scientist,known for his work in india's space and missile programs.

Achievements

  • Developed India's first satillite launch vehicle(SLV-III)
  • Led India's successful nuclear tests in 1998
  • Wrote books like "Wings of Fire" and "India and 2020"
  • Received the Bharat Ratna,India's highest civilian award
